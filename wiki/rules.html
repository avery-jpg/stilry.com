That means you’re streaming to close to nobody or nobody. ### Rude It’s simple, just don’t be rude. If you do you’ll risk being banned from watching my streams and videos. ### Mod Disobeying Short and simple; don’t disobey the mods as they have permission to, also, ban you from my channel(s) based on your actions. ### Swearing Swearing is allowed, but not to or about people. An example is if you’re saying something like “S*it dude that was amazing!” but ***never*** something like “F*ck you” (to someone). Me personally won’t swear so I can stay kid-friendly. ### Links Do not post links unless given permission to by a mod. Any links that are made by/accepted by me are also allowed, of course. ### Discriminatory Remarks Don’t say ***any*** form of racist, homophobic, transphobic, sexist, or anything in that category. ### Leaking This is pretty obvious. Don’t leak anyone’s private photos, links, phone numbers, emails, addresses, or anything you wouldn’t want to share. ### Judgement For any rules not listed here, use your knowledge I know you have. Of course, you can ask if so and so is against the rules or not and give an example ***if asked***, without getting punished. ### Inappropriate Discussions You ***are not*** allowed to engage in inappropriate discussions such as genitalia (specific body parts), or anything to do with intimacy. ### Pinging Do not mass ping people in the (Twitch) chat, and for YouTube if they ever have that. ### Impersonation Do not impersonate viewers, mods, or any streamers, popular or not. ### Threatening Easy enough, don’t threaten people. Doesn’t matter what, just don’t. ### Administrative Decisions Any administrative decisions are final and cannot be argued against. ### Raiding You are allowed to raid my live streams, as long as nobody breaks any rule. ### Appealing Me and/or my mods will get to your appeal as soon as possible, don’t spam in chat to have me/the mods to look at your appeal or your appeal will be declined. ### Spamming You are not allowed to spam unless, unless I say you can.
